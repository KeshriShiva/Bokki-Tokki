# 🍜 Bokki Tokki

A modern **self-service kiosk web application** for a **Quick Service Restaurant (QSR)** that enables customers to browse the menu, customize their orders, manage their cart, and place orders through an intuitive digital interface. The project aims to simplify the in-store ordering experience while reducing customer waiting time.

---

## 📖 Overview

Bokki Tokki replicates the ordering experience commonly found in modern fast-food restaurants. Instead of ordering at a counter, customers can interact with a digital kiosk to explore menu items, add products to their cart, review their selections, and complete their order seamlessly.

The application is designed with a clean and responsive interface, ensuring a smooth user experience across desktop and mobile devices.
